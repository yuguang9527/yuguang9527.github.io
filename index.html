About

I am deeply passionate about advancing the fields of generative modeling, embodied AI, and robotics. I developed a strong foundation in electronic engineering at Osaka University, which I applied during my time at Uber Japan leading robotics and autonomous delivery projects. I have collaborated with researchers at Stanford exploring the integration of generative models with real-world applications. I aim to further develop scalable machine learning systems and multimodal generative models while contributing to research that bridges perception and action in intelligent systems.
